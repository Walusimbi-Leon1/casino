🎰 Casino Night — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Casino Night ("the Game", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you play.

1. Information We Collect

When you play Casino Night, we collect the following information:

  • Discord User ID — a unique numeric identifier for your Discord account (if playing via Discord).
  • Display Name — the name you enter or your Discord username, shown publicly on the leaderboard and in chat.
  • Game State — your in-game balance, spin activity, and session presence (used to show who is currently playing).
  • Chat Messages — messages you send in the live chat, visible to all active players.
  • Anonymised Player ID — a unique anonymous identifier generated if you play in browser mode without Discord.

We do not collect your email address, IP address (beyond what Firebase requires for its normal operations), real name, or any Discord guild/server information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To persist your game balance across sessions.
  • To display the leaderboard (player name + balance).
  • To enable live chat between players.
  • To detect active/inactive players for leaderboard cleanup.

Your information is only used for the operation of the Game. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Game data is stored in a Firebase Realtime Database (Google Cloud, us-central1). Data is organised under the casino/ path prefix and includes:

  • casino/players/{playerId}/ — name, balance, lastSeen timestamp.
  • casino/chat/ — chat messages with author ID, name, text, and timestamp.

Firebase's security and privacy practices apply to the underlying infrastructure. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

4. Discord OAuth

If you play via Discord's Activity feature, we use Discord OAuth2 to obtain:

  • Your Discord User ID
  • Your Discord username and avatar

We do not request or receive access tokens for Discord API actions on your behalf. The OAuth token is exchanged server-side (via Cloudflare Functions) and never stored. Authentication is solely for identifying you within the Game.

5. Data Retention

Player data is retained while you are active in the Game. If you do not play for an extended period, your data may remain in the database. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us (see Section 8).

Chat messages older than approximately 48 hours may be automatically pruned.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

7. Children's Privacy

Casino Night is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.
